About OrthoTrack

Revolutionizing fracture detection with the power of Artificial Intelligence.

What is OrthoTrack?

OrthoTrack is a powerful AI-driven web application designed to detect fractures in arm X-ray images within seconds. Our system uses YOLOv8 for image processing and Flask for web deployment, ensuring real-time and accurate detection.

The primary goal of OrthoTrack is to assist healthcare professionals and reduce diagnosis time, especially in remote healthcare centers.
